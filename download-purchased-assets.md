# 🐌 Download purchased assets

How to Download a Script Purchased via Keymaster

1. **Log in to your CFX.re Account:**
   * Go to [Keymaster Login](https://keymaster.fivem.net/asset-grants).
   * Enter your credentials and log in.
2. **Navigate to Granted Assets:**
   * Select "Granted Assets" from the navigation menu on the left side.
3. **Find your Purchased Script:**
   * In the "Granted Assets" section, locate the script you have purchased.
4. **Download the Script:**
   * Press the blue "Download" button to download the asset.
5. **Install the Script on Your Server:**
   * Follow the installation instructions provided in the script documentation.
   * Ensure you follow any specific steps required for the FiveM escrow system if applicable.
