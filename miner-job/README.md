# ⛏️ Miner job

Boost your FiveM server with our MINER JOB script, designed for a dynamic mining experience. This script features a custom UI, allowing players to choose from 3 unique clothing styles and 2 rental vehicle options. Progress through a comprehensive level and XP system, earning percentage-based bonuses for XP and cash as you level up. Engage in challenging quests, work solo or in teams, and enjoy immersive gameplay with custom props and sounds. Tailored for seamless integration, the MINER JOB script offers a polished and engaging role-play experience for all players.
